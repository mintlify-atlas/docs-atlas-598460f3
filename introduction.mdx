  <Steps>
    <Step title="Create your account">
      Sign up for SIGSAHO and configure your organization profile. You'll receive admin credentials to access the system.
      
      ```bash
      POST /auth/register
      ```
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Configure your first aircraft">
      Add aircraft to your fleet with registration details, maintenance schedules, and operational parameters.
      
      ```typescript
      {
        "registration": "XA-ABC",
        "model": "Bell 407",
        "status": "operational",
        "maxFlightHours": 2500
      }
      ```
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Set up user roles and permissions">
      Configure role-based access control for your team members including pilots, dispatchers, and maintenance staff.
      
      <Note>
        Default roles include: Administrator, Dispatcher, Pilot, Maintenance Crew, and Observer.
      </Note>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Create your first medical transfer">
      Begin coordinating medical transfers with patient information, crew assignments, and route planning.
      
      ```typescript
      {
        "patientName": "John Doe",
        "origin": "Hospital A",
        "destination": "Hospital B",
        "aircraftId": "abc-123",
        "priority": "urgent"
      }
      ```
    </Step>
  </Steps>
</div>
