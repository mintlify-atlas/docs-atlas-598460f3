## Overview

The Aircraft Management module provides complete lifecycle management for your aviation fleet. Track technical specifications, monitor flight hours, schedule maintenance, and maintain regulatory compliance for all aircraft in your organization.

<Note>
  All aircraft data integrates seamlessly with flight operations, automatically updating flight hours, cycles, and maintenance schedules.
</Note>

## User Workflows

### Registering a New Aircraft

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Aircraft Configuration">
    Navigate to **Configuración > Aeronaves** from the main menu. The system displays your current fleet with status indicators and maintenance alerts.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Aircraft Record">
    Click **"Nueva Aeronave"** to open the aircraft registration form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Basic Information">
    Complete the aircraft identification:
    
    **Identification**
    - Aircraft name (e.g., "BELL 407")
    - Registration/Tail number (e.g., "XC-XAO")
    - Description
    - Aircraft category (Rotary Wing / Fixed Wing)
    
    **Manufacturer Details**
    - Manufacturer name
    - Model designation
    - Number of engines (1 or 2)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Technical Specifications">
    Enter weight and balance parameters:
    
    **Empty Weight Configuration**
    - Weight empty (lbs)
    - Center of gravity empty (inches)
    - Moment empty (lb-in)
    - Maximum passengers capacity
    
    **Engine Limits**
    - Time limit motor 1 (hours)
    - Time limit motor 2 (hours, if applicable)
    - Cycle limit motor 1
    - Cycle limit motor 2 (if applicable)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Maintenance Parameters">
    Configure maintenance tracking:
    
    **Current Status**
    - Current flight hours accumulated
    - Current cycles accumulated
    - Date of last major maintenance
    
    **Lifetime Limits**
    - Total lifetime hours (hours before next major maintenance)
    - Hours accumulated before last maintenance
    - Total lifetime cycles
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload Aircraft Image">
    Upload a photo of the aircraft:
    - Supported formats: JPEG, PNG
    - Recommended size: 1920x1080px
    - Images are automatically compressed for optimal performance
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Initial Status">
    Select the aircraft's operational status:
    - **Active**: Available for flight operations
    - **Maintenance**: Undergoing scheduled or unscheduled maintenance
    - **Out of Service**: Temporarily unavailable
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Aircraft Dispatch Workflow

The dispatch page provides real-time operational data for each aircraft:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Aircraft Dispatch">
    Navigate to **Despacho > [Aircraft Name]** to view the aircraft-specific dispatch dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Flight Status">
    The dashboard displays three critical metrics:
    
    **Available Hours**
    - Hours remaining until next scheduled maintenance
    - Color-coded urgency indicator
    - Formatted as hours and minutes
    
    **Current Accumulated Hours**
    - Total flight time logged
    - Includes historical hours from previous maintenance cycles
    
    **Next Inspection Due**
    - Total lifetime hours limit
    - Percentage of hours consumed
    - Color-coded alert level:
      - 🟢 **Green (0-49%)**: Within safe operating range
      - 🟡 **Amber (50-79%)**: Schedule maintenance soon
      - 🔴 **Red (80-100%)**: Critical - maintenance required
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Recent Flight Records">
    Access the flight history table showing:
    - Recent transfers and operations
    - Flight hours logged per mission
    - Fuel consumption records
    - Crew assignments
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Perform Dispatch Actions">
    From the dispatch page, you can:
    - Create new weight & balance calculation
    - View aircraft documents
    - Access maintenance records
    - Register new service/inspection
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

### Automatic Maintenance Tracking

<Card title="Real-Time Flight Hour Tracking" icon="clock">
  **Automatic Hour Accumulation**
  
  Every time a flight operation is logged:
  
  1. Flight duration is automatically added to `current_time_flying`
  2. System recalculates `available_time` (hours until maintenance)
  3. Maintenance due percentage is updated
  4. Dashboard alerts are triggered if thresholds exceeded
  
  **Virtual Getters:**
  - `available_time = total_lifetime_hours - current_time_flying`
  - `maintenance_due_percentage = (hours_used / total_interval) × 100`
  
  No manual intervention required - the system maintains accuracy automatically.
</Card>

### Multi-Engine Support

<Card title="Independent Engine Tracking" icon="cog">
  **Dual Engine Configuration**
  
  For multi-engine aircraft, SIGSAHO tracks each engine independently:
  
  - **Motor 1**: Primary engine time and cycle limits
  - **Motor 2**: Secondary engine time and cycle limits (optional)
  
  Each engine has:
  - Time limit (hours)
  - Cycle limit (number of start/stop cycles)
  - Independent maintenance schedules
  
  System alerts when either engine approaches limits.
</Card>

### Weight and Balance Integration

<Card title="Technical Specifications for W&B" icon="balance-scale">
  **Pre-Configured Parameters**
  
  Aircraft weight and balance data is used for:
  
  - Automatic empty weight calculation
  - Center of gravity (CG) validation
  - Maximum takeoff weight enforcement
  - Passenger capacity limits
  
  When creating a new weight & balance sheet, the system:
  1. Pre-fills aircraft empty weight, CG, and moment
  2. Validates total weight against aircraft limits
  3. Ensures CG remains within safe envelope
  4. Prevents unsafe loading configurations
</Card>

## Soft Deletion and Audit Trail

<Warning>
  Deleting an aircraft performs a **soft delete** - the record is marked as "Eliminated" but retained for audit purposes.
</Warning>

**Soft Deletion Behavior:**

1. User clicks "Delete" on aircraft
2. System prompts for confirmation
3. Aircraft `status` is set to `ELIMINATED`
4. `deleted_at` timestamp is recorded
5. Aircraft removed from active fleet views
6. Aircraft appears in "Historial de Bajas" (Deletion History)

**Audit Trail Access:**
- Navigate to **Configuración > Aeronaves**
- Click **"Historial de Bajas"** toggle
- View all soft-deleted aircraft with deletion dates
- Records are read-only for compliance

**Data Retention:**
- Flight records remain linked to aircraft
- Maintenance history preserved
- Weight & balance records intact
- Referenced in historical reports

## Best Practices

<Accordion title="Accurate Hour Tracking">
  - Verify initial hours when registering aircraft
  - Cross-check hours with aircraft logbook
  - Update maintenance limits after major overhauls
  - Monitor hour accumulation monthly
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Proactive Maintenance Scheduling">
  - Schedule maintenance when at 70-80% of limits
  - Don't wait for red alerts (80%+)
  - Plan maintenance during low-demand periods
  - Keep maintenance window buffer for unexpected issues
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Weight and Balance Accuracy">
  - Update empty weight after major modifications
  - Verify CG shifts after equipment changes
  - Recalculate moment after weight changes
  - Re-weigh aircraft annually per regulations
</Accordion>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Available Hours Showing Negative">
  **Cause:** Current hours exceed total lifetime hours (maintenance overdue)
  
  **Solution:**
  1. Ground aircraft immediately
  2. Schedule required maintenance
  3. After maintenance, update `total_lifetime_hours` and reset `hours_before_lastMantt`
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Maintenance Percentage Not Updating">
  **Cause:** Flight operations not properly closing/logging hours
  
  **Solution:**
  1. Verify recent transfers have status "Finished"
  2. Check aircraft assignment in transfer records
  3. Manually refresh aircraft page
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cannot Set Aircraft to Active">
  **Cause:** Aircraft may have overdue maintenance or missing required documents
  
  **Solution:**
  1. Check maintenance due percentage (must be < 100%)
  2. Verify all required documents uploaded
  3. Ensure no open discrepancies
</Accordion>
